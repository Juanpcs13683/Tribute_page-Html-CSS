Tejo The Colombian Sport

Game to the Turmequé (Juego al turmequé) Well-known as the Colombian Sport.

History

Tejo (Spanish pronunciation: [te.ho]), also known, to a lesser degree, as turmequé (pronounced [tuɾ.meke]), is a traditional throwing sport in Colombia. It is characteristic for its use of small targets containing gunpowder, which explode on impact.

Cultural Background

Tejo is a sport that is very popular among the general public. Although there are some Tejo arenas that cater to members of the higher socioeconomic classes, most of them are made and used by the popular class who consider them to be portraits of their pre-Columbian origins. Only football has more followers and a developed industry in the country.
